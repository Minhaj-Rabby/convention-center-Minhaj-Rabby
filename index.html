Frequently Asked Questions

Please visit our online event calendar. While the majority of our conventions, trade shows and consumer shows are listed, please note that private banquets and meetings are typically not displayed, along with events that our clients prefer to keep confidential.

Our regular business hours are 8:30 am to 5:00 pm Monday through Friday. If you require access to the building outside of these hours, please contact Guest Services for assistance.

While we do not offer public access to the living roof, it is designed to be visible both inside and outside the building. We recommend that you visit the Jack Poole Plaza at the Convention Centre. There is a pathway on top of the Cactus Club restaurant, where an extension of the living roof is planted.

We offer a public tour program on select days, subject to availability and building closures. Please visit Public Tours for more information.
